# 🏢 BCHSoftware Corporate Standard & Associate Orientation Manual
### Document ID: HR-SOP-2026
**Corporate Compliance and Workplace Operations | Version 1.0**

Welcome to the team. By onboarding at BCHSoftware, you are transitioning from a traditional student to a **Junior Associate**. In this workspace, we do not have "assignments"; we deliver **Project Milestones**. We do not have "school rules"; we adhere to **Corporate Policy**. 

Your advancement, compensation, and workplace standing directly determine your academic performance evaluation.

---

## 💸 1. The Discretionary ClarkeCoin Economy

Every active associate is provisioned a starting discretionary balance of **2,000 ClarkeCoin (CC)** on the company ledger. This currency exists to model financial literacy and professional resource management.

### Base Compensation & Cost of Living:
* **The Base Stipend:** Associates receive a baseline salary processing transaction every week, determined by their actively certified role and sprint contributions.
* **The Fixed Living Expense:** Every Monday morning, a fixed **Living Expense Bundle fee of 250 CC** is automatically deducted from your ledger balance to cover your workspace node footprint (desk rent, cloud connectivity, utilities).
* **The Mainframe Maintenance Fee:** To simulate cloud architecture costs, the corporate server charges an automated **5 CC fee** for every log entry or work ticket processing action.

### The Performance Improvement Plan (PIP):
If an associate's discretionary balance drops below zero due to chronic policy fines or missed milestones, they are automatically placed on a **Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)**. A PIP represents an academic recovery state and requires immediate remediation with the Chief Technology Officer (CTO) to regain financial and academic standing.

---

## ⚠️ 2. Workplace Conduct & Compliance Fines (HR Regulations)

To maintain a high-velocity, professional engineering environment, the CTO enforces strict compliance standards. Infractions represent a drain on company resources and result in immediate deductions logged directly to your financial profile:

* **Workstation Asset Misuse:** Company terminals are strictly for project development, documentation, and research. Accessing video games, streaming media, or non-work-related browsing triggers a **-50 CC Corporate Infraction Fine**.
* **Focus Protocol Violation:** Personal mobile communication devices must remain out of sight and stored in Focus Mode during active development sprints. A visible mobile device on an active workspace desk triggers a **-25 CC Security Fine**.
* **Meeting Tardiness:** Punctuality is mandatory for the daily morning Stand-up. Arriving late to your team's sync disrupts the engineering pipeline and triggers a **-10 CC fine per minute**.

---

## 🛠️ 3. Software Integrity & Corporate Ethics

BCHSoftware values data integrity and authenticity above all else. 

* **Corporate Espionage (Plagiarism):** Copying another associate's code or blueprints without explicit collaboration clearance is a violation of our ethical charter. It results in an immediate freeze on your payroll ledger and a formal security audit.
* **AI Copilot Compliance:** Generative AI tools are to be treated as a junior assistant for research and debugging error codes. Blindly copy-pasting code blocks without being able to textually explain the underlying logic is prohibited. Every associate must be prepared to verbally defend their logic during live **CTO Code Audits**.

---

## 📈 4. Corporate Performance Tiers (Grading Matrix)

Your end-of-cycle performance evaluation translates your corporate standing into your official academic mark:

* **Senior Associate (Performance Tier: A):** Consistently executes clean, verified milestones before the sprint clock expires. Maintains a highly positive ledger balance, actively cross-trains in secondary roles, and contributes exceptional peer-review documentation.
* **Staff Associate (Performance Tier: B):** Successfully delivers functional, bug-free codebase assets to the mainframe. Adheres to all workspace compliance protocols and maintains a stable financial trajectory.
* **Junior Associate (Performance Tier: C/D):** Experiences systematic deployment bottlenecks. Requires significant technical support or manager intervention to clear basic syntax errors; experiences ledger strain due to repeated conduct fines.
