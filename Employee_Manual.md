* [cite_start]**Consumables Lock:** No food or liquids (except water in a securely sealed container away from hardware components) are permitted at any individual workstation[cite: 19].

* [cite_start]**Cell Phones (Proprietary Data Protection):** To preserve trade secrets and maximize development time, personal mobile devices must be silenced and stored entirely inside your backpack before taking your seat[cite: 25]. [cite_start]Phones must remain out of sight for the duration of the entire shift[cite: 26]. [cite_start]Unauthorized device visibility or usage will result in a Minor Offense[cite: 27].
* [cite_start]**Headphone & Earbud Policy:** The use of headphones, earbuds, or headsets is permitted only under the following professional compliance parameters[cite: 28]:
  * [cite_start]*Approved Hardware:* Audio equipment may only be connected directly to company-issued hardware (Laptops or Chromebooks)[cite: 29].
  * [cite_start]*Approved Use Case:* Canvas viewing class material or completing individual role-certification modules[cite: 30].
